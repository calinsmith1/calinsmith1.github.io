Final Project

This is the final project for CSC 106 called Dish-Dash

Dish - Dash

In Dish-Dash, the main goal of the game is to complete as many orders as possible in the given amount of time, 60 seconds. Use the WASD keys to move and the mouse to click on the ingredients when near them. Start the game by clicking “Start Order” where you will then get a random customer asking you for certain items. Each Item completed earns you 50 points. Click on “How to Play” on the game screen to see these instructions again. You may also choose your character which are the two developers of the game Faylynn Vidrio and Calin Smith. Challenge your friends and family to see who earns the highest score and have fun!
